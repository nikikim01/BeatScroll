How this works

Quick start

  1. Paste or type your text, then click Load Text.
  2. Click anywhere outside the textarea, then press ASDF or JKL; — each press moves you one syllable further into the word under the dashed line.
  3. That's the whole loop. Everything below is optional flavor.

Add rhythm (optional — pure sound, doesn't move your place)

Space kick · H hi-hat · Shift+H open hi-hat · V snare · C cowbell · X stick click. Hold P / Q while playing a note to shift its pitch up or down an octave.

Adjust your pace (optional)

The Presses per syllable slider raises how many taps each syllable needs — higher is slower and more deliberate. Hold Shift (right) while pressing note keys to burst forward through easy stretches — the longer you hold, the bigger each press's jump. The Gradual deceleration checkbox decides whether letting go snaps you back to your normal pace instantly or eases you back down.
