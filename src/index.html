BeatScroll MVP keys = sound · presses = progress

Press ASDF or JKL; to advance the text.

Each press “pays” one syllable-step. When the word’s pips fill, it advances to the next word. (Syllables are approximate in this MVP.)

Controls

Space Kick (timekeeping only)
H Hi-hat (closed)
Shift + H Hi-hat (open)
V Snare
A S D F J K L ; White keys + advance 1 syllable
W E R U I Black keys + advance 1 syllable
P Hold = Octave up
Q Hold = Octave down
